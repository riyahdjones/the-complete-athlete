Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

The Complete Athlete helps athletes build goals, track daily productivity, complete performance plans, journal, receive parent support, and use mindset coaching. We collect the information needed to provide those features, keep accounts secure, and improve the app experience.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, role, and login information.
  • Athlete profile details such as sport, age, state or country, profile photo, and parent access code.
  • App activity such as goals, productivity tasks, check-ins, plan progress, points, streaks, and journal entries.
  • AI coach messages, usage limits, and limited coaching memory used to support future performance conversations.
  • Notification information such as device tokens, notification preferences, and notification delivery history.
  • Technical information such as app version, device type, diagnostics, and basic event logs used to maintain reliability and safety.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide the app experience, save progress, link parents to athletes, power the AI coach, enforce safety and usage limits, send requested notifications, troubleshoot issues, and support account requests.

Parent Visibility

Parents can only view athlete information after linking through the parent access flow. Parent-facing views are designed for support and accountability. Journal entries and AI coach chats are treated as private unless the product clearly provides a sharing option.

AI Coach

The AI coach supports mental performance, reflection, confidence, and growth. It is not therapy, medical care, emergency support, or a substitute for parents, guardians, coaches, athletic trainers, doctors, counselors, or other qualified professionals. AI responses may be incomplete or inaccurate, so users should use judgment and involve a trusted adult for serious matters.

Notifications

If notifications are enabled, we may send Daily Deposits, new performance plan alerts, streak reminders, and account-related messages. You can turn notifications off in the app or through your device settings.

Sharing And Service Providers

We do not sell personal information. We use trusted service providers such as Supabase, Vercel, OpenAI, and Apple push notification services to operate core app features. These providers process information only as needed to deliver the services we use.

Data Storage And Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect account data. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, but we limit access, use authentication controls, and monitor important backend health signals.

Children And Families

The app is built for athletes and families. Young athletes should use the app with parent or guardian awareness. Parents or guardians may contact us about access, support, or deletion questions.

Account Deletion

You can request account deletion inside the app from the Profile area or by contacting support. Deleting an account removes the user account and associated app records where possible, except information we may need to retain for security, legal, or operational reasons.

Contact

For privacy, support, or deletion help, contact: support@thecompleteathlete.app
