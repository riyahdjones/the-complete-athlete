Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

The Complete Athlete helps athletes build goals, standards, reflection habits, performance plans, parent accountability, and mindset coaching. We collect only the information needed to run those features.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as name, email, role, and login details.
  • Athlete profile details such as sport, age, location, profile photo, and parent access code.
  • App activity such as goals, daily standards, readiness check-ins, plan progress, points, and journal entries.
  • AI coach messages and limited coach memory used to support future performance conversations.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide the app experience, save progress, link parents to athletes, power the AI coach, maintain safety limits, improve reliability, and support account requests.

Parent Visibility

Parents can only view data for athletes they are linked to through an access code. Journal entries and AI coach chats are treated as private unless the product explicitly provides a sharing option.

AI Coach

The AI coach supports mental performance and personal growth. It is not therapy, medical care, emergency support, or a substitute for trusted adults or qualified professionals.

Data Storage And Security

We use Supabase and Vercel to store and operate app data. Access controls are designed so athletes manage their own data and linked parents can only read approved athlete information.

Account Deletion

You can request account deletion inside the app from Account & Safety. Deleting an account removes the user account and associated app records where possible.

Contact

For privacy, support, or deletion help, contact: support@thecompleteathlete.app
