Common Requests

  • Password reset: use Reset Password on the login screen.
  • Account deletion: sign in, open Account & Safety, then choose Delete Account.
  • Parent access: ask the athlete for the parent access code from their profile.
  • AI coach safety: for danger, injury, self-harm, abuse, or emergencies, contact a trusted adult or emergency services immediately.
